About

Welcome to Danielle's Art website! I hope you enjoy your stay. Here are some instructions to help you get started. You can navigate between pages using the navigation bar above. The Home page brings you back here.


Some of the pages on the website are split by categories. Each category of artwork has descriptions on the page itself to explain the content that is being shown. An example for DC, the character's name and description of who they are will be included.

The Home and Suggestions pages are exceptions to this. Home brings you back here and Suggestions lets registered users suggest new drawings and categories.
